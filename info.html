Is Automated Schedule Solving Right for Your Group?

Scheduling can be improved by the use of software in a number of ways, but the crucial check for whether "Schedule Solve" is right as a solution is a schedule's complexity. When the number of employees, shifts, and length of schedule reaches a certain size, the exercise of assigning employees to shifts becomes impossibly complex.
If your schedule has all these elements, Schedule Solve is right for you:

  • Defined lists of shifts and staff before each schedule.
  • Rules for whether a schedule's arrangement is acceptable or not.
  • No satisfactory method for satisfying those rules in a reasonable amount of time.
