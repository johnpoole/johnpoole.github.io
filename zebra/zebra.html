About

Zebra Planner creates optimal schedule arrangements using two main components.

The first component is the definition of what the best schedule is. For existing clients, that has been based on equity of schedules, accommodation of sabbaticals, special cases for seniority, sufficient rest between groups of assignments and regular predicable patterns.

The second component is a software process that takes about 24 hours to check all the possible arrangements of physicians and assignments, measuring each arrangement against the definition, and then reporting on which one is the best.
